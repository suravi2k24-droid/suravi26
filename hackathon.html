Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the hackathon?

The hackathon is open to all college students (undergraduate and postgraduate) from any discipline. Inter-college teams are allowed.

What is the team size?

Teams should have 2-4 members. Solo participants are not allowed. You can register as a team or we can help you find team members.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, chargers, any hardware you plan to use, valid college ID, and lots of energy! Food and refreshments will be provided.

What are the judging criteria?

Projects will be judged on innovation (30%), technical implementation (30%), design/usability (20%), and presentation (20%).

Is there a registration fee?

Registration is completely free! We provide food, swag, and prizes at no cost to participants.

Can we use pre-built code?

You can use open-source libraries and frameworks, but the core code must be written during the hackathon. Pre-built templates are not allowed.
