About GIET

Gandhi Institute of Excellent Technocrats (GIET), Ghangapatna, is a NAAC A+ accredited and NBA accredited premier institute in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, recognized for its academic excellence, advanced research, and industry-oriented education. The institute is dedicated to shaping future leaders in engineering, technology, and management through global standards in education and strong industry collaborations.GIET Ghangapatna is also renowned for SURAVI, its flagship techno-cultural fest, where creativity, technology, and talent converge to create an unforgettable experience.
