About SURAVI-2K26

SURAVI-2K26 is the annual techno-cultural extravaganza of GIET, where innovation meets creativity, and passion fuels excellence! It's a grand celebration that brings together the brightest minds, the most talented performers, and the most enthusiastic participants in a festival of technology, culture, and limitless possibilities.From electrifying competitions and hackathons to mesmerizing music, dance, and art, SURAVI-2K26 is more than just an event—it's a movement, an experience, and a legacy in the making. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, an artist, or a dreamer, this is your stage to shine, create, and inspire. Get ready to ignite your spirit, push your limits, and make memories that last a lifetime!

About GIET

Gandhi Institute of Excellent Technocrats (GIET), Ghangapatna, is a NAAC A+ accredited and NBA accredited premier institute in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, recognized for its academic excellence, advanced research, and industry-oriented education. The institute is dedicated to shaping future leaders in engineering, technology, and management through global standards in education and strong industry collaborations.GIET Ghangapatna is also renowned for SURAVI, its flagship techno-cultural fest, where creativity, technology, and talent converge to create an unforgettable experience. Learn. Innovate. Excel. Welcome to GIET, where the future begins!
