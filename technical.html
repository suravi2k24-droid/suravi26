Engineering & Hardware Events

7 Events

Model Fiesta

Participants build innovative working models showcasing engineering or scientific principles. Rules: Models must be original and self-constructed. Participants must present a demonstration and explain its functionality. No pre-built kits allowed. Judging Criteria: Innovation, feasibility, working mechanism, and presentation. Excitement Factor: Witnessing futuristic models and prototypes in action.

Waste-O-Craft

Transform waste materials into creative and useful products. Sustainability meets innovation in this eco-friendly competition.

Mega Structure

Build large, load-bearing structures using basic materials. Rules: No pre-assembled structures allowed. Time limit: 2 hours. Judging Criteria: Strength, design, and durability. Excitement Factor: Testing structures under weight loads until they collapse!

Circuitrix

Solve electrical circuit-related problems. Rules: Time limit: 30 minutes per circuit. Debugging tasks may be included. Judging Criteria: Accuracy, speed, and efficiency. Excitement Factor: Seeing circuits come alive through light bulbs, buzzers, and motors..

ROBOTRONIX

Design and program robots for various tasks (e.g., maze solving, battle, sumo wrestling). Rules: No off-the-shelf robots. Must follow pre-defined arena rules. Judging Criteria: Functionality, automation, and speed. Excitement Factor: Robots battling and performing impressive feats!.

Udaan

Build and fly model aircraft or drones. Rules: Weight restrictions apply. Time limit: 3 minutes per flight. Judging Criteria: Flight duration, stability, and aerodynamics. Excitement Factor: Watching DIY planes soar or crash spectacularly.

Poster Presentation

Do you have the vision to explore, innovate, and inspire? Tech Talk is the perfect stage to showcase your expertise, ideas, and insights on cutting-edge technologies and groundbreaking innovations. Engage in thought-provoking discussions, present your perspectives, and captivate the audience with your knowledge. This event is designed to test your articulation, technical understanding, and ability to think on your feet. Only the most compelling speakers will leave a lasting impact.

Coding & Programming Events

6 Events

Source Code

The source code challenge for the Suravi Tech Cultural Fest emphasizes writing clean, efficient, and well-structured code. Participants will be judged on code quality, readability, optimization, and adherence to best practices. Key factors include proper commenting, modularity, and efficient algorithms. The participant who submits the most organized, maintainable, and performant source code will win the competition.

Code-O-Soccors

Get ready to merge coding with the thrill of soccer in Codo-O-Soccor, an exhilarating event where strategy, logic, and precision come together on the virtual field. Program your bot to play, pass, and score against opponents in a high-intensity coding battle. Your ability to write efficient algorithms, predict moves, and adapt in real time will determine your victory. Only the smartest coders with the best game strategy will take home the win. Do you have what it takes?

Blind Coding

Think you can code without seeing what you type? Blind Coding is the ultimate test of your programming skills, logic, and muscle memory. With your screen hidden, you must rely solely on your knowledge and accuracy to write error-free code. This challenge pushes the boundaries of concentration, precision, and confidence, rewarding only those who can truly code with their eyes closed. Are you up for it?

Web War

Prepare for an intense battle of creativity, speed, and technical prowess in Web War, a competition where web developers clash to design and build the most efficient, visually stunning, and functional websites. Whether itâ€™s front-end aesthetics, back-end logic, or full-stack innovation, this event will push your web development skills to the limit. Compete against the best and prove that your code reigns supreme in the digital battlefield!

Mobile App Development

The mobile application development challenge for the Suravi Tech Cultural Fest invites participants to build the best-performing mobile app. The competition will focus on app functionality, user interface, performance, and innovation. Participants will be judged on aspects like usability, responsiveness, and design coherence. The app that demonstrates the most seamless functionality, creativity, and user-centric design will be declared the winner.

Startup Fair

The Start-Up Fair challenge at the Suravi Tech Cultural Fest invites aspiring entrepreneurs to pitch their innovative business ideas. Participants will be judged on the uniqueness, feasibility, and potential impact of their startup concepts. Key criteria include market potential, business model, and scalability. Idea-con

Brainstorm and present innovative technological solutions. Rules: Individual or team participation. Time limit: 5-minute presentation. Judging Criteria: Feasibility, innovation, and impact. Excitement Factor: Groundbreaking ideas for the future.

Hackathon

The GIET Hackathon is a competitive, collaborative event that invites students to solve real-world problems through innovative coding and software development. This guideline provides a structured approach for organizing and executing the hackathon, from registration to the final evaluation.
